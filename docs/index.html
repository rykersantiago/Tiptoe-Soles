About Us

Who we are and what we stand for

TipToe Soles is an Australian owned and operated company. We aim to provide comfortable footwear at affordable prices to customers within Australia and around the world. Our first range of products focuses on comfort and daily wear shoes, designed for those aged 30 and above who value quality and practicality.

Our Mission

To be the go-to affordable footwear brand in Australia and internationally — offering comfort, style and value in every pair.

Our Products

Our debut range includes carefully crafted men's and women's shoes with features like orthotic-friendly insoles, breathable mesh linings, and durable rubber soles.

Policies

Our commitments to you

Return Policy

FREE RETURNS on all items over $60 within 28 days of the order date.

TipToe Soles is confident that you will be happy with your purchase. If for any reason this is not the case, we offer free returns for items over $60 within 28 days of the order date.

To be eligible for a return, items must:

  • Be returned within 28 days of the order date
  • Be unworn with original tags still attached
  • Have no dirty marks or damaged soles
  • Be returned in the original, undamaged shoe box

Please note: postage is non-refundable. TipToe Soles will not take responsibility for returned items not received if registered post is not used.

Exchange Policy

We want you to love your TipToe Soles purchase. If you need a different size or colour, we're happy to assist with an exchange within 28 days of purchase.

Items exchanged must meet the same conditions as our returns policy — unworn, original tags, original packaging. Contact our customer service team to arrange your exchange.

Delivery Policy

Orders placed before noon AEST will be packed and dispatched on business days via Australia Post.

Express Shipping:

  • 1–2 business days — Victoria

  • 2–5 business days — WA, NT, Tasmania
